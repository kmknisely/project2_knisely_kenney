Church of the Beloved is a church plant in the Diocese of RI. We meet every Sunday evening at 5pm for a casual worship service in Pascoag, RI.

You are Beloved

Everyone and anyone is invited to worship with us each week. Families and children are welcome, and we encourage you to dress comfortably. Our worship comes from the Book of Common Prayer, we celebrate the Holy Eucharist each week, and our music is accompanied by guitar, piano, and drum.
